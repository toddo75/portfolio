About Me

Hello, my name is Todd J. Savard and I was born and raised in Michigan's beautiful Upper Peninsula. I have spent the majority of my life working in the food and beverage industry. I have learned a great deal about myself and life working in that industry. It has taught me patience, acceptance and empathy, to name a few. I am quite thankful for the years I spent there, whether it be bartending and waiting table, or or grilling steaks on the line in the back of the house, and everything in between. Alas, at nearly 50 years of age, my time in the food and beverage industry has come to an end. I feel the need to spread my wings, so to say. I have set my sights on web development and coding industry. I look forward to this new challenge and cannot wait to see what the future may hold.
